About Us

We're like build-a-bear, but instead of you having to put in the work, we build 'em for you!
Seriously, we'll take any shape (doesn't even have to be a bear), stuff it full of fluff, and ship it to you.

We were established with love in early 2022, with the sole intent of bringing the happiness and joy we feel to everyone in the form of soft toys.
We're an entirely-online store and our warehouse is automated and/or operated remotely - no danger for our employees!
